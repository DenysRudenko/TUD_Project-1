James

James is the main character he is 29, introverted and has a massive temper. Nothing comes in the way of James finding his missing wife.

Laura is 8 and holds a strong grudge against James. She knew James's wife making James eager to talk to her.

Maria

Maria is almost identical to James's missing wife Mary causing James to get her confused with Mary. She helps James in his journey to find his wife, but she might know a lot more then she claims.
